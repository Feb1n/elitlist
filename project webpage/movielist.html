Overview
For my project I am aiming to make a program that helps users keep track of the TV shows and movies that they are watching. This program would create lists for users, separated into watched, watching and planning to watch, where they could track the number of episodes they watched, give the show a personal rating, track their statistics or get recommendations from the program based on the genres they like and also based on the shows that other users on the system have watched.
Features
Watchlist
Seperate your list into Watched, Watching and Planning and update their status freely

Statistics
Track your Watchtime, Episodes finished and favoutite genres with all on one page

Recommendations
Using our specialised algorithim, we will provide Recommendations that hand picked for you

Multiple users
Have multiple accounts on the same device
